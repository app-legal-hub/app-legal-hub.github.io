# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 13, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Fitness Tracker Pro ("Application") protects user privacy and what data is processed for what purposes.

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

- Name and email address
- Physical characteristics (height, weight, age)
- Workout and activity data
- Location data (for route tracking)
- Device information
- Usage statistics and app interactions

## 3. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you wish to delete your account, you can do so from the app settings. Upon account deletion, your data will be removed from our active systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes.
